## Bot Not Joining My Meeting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The bot isn't joining">
    **Try these steps:**
    - Make sure the meeting has started (at least one person must be in it)
    - Check that the meeting link is correct and complete
    - Verify you're using a supported platform (Google Meet or Microsoft Teams)
    - Try using **Instant Join** to manually send the bot
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bot stuck in waiting room">
    **Solution:** The meeting host needs to admit the bot.
    
    - For **Google Meet**: Look for "Someone wants to join" notification and click **Admit**
    - For **Microsoft Teams**: Check the lobby and click **Admit** next to the bot
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bot was removed from the meeting">
    **Possible causes:**
    - The host may have kicked the bot
    - Meeting security settings may block guests
    - For Teams, guest access may be disabled
    
    **Solution:** Ask the meeting host to allow the bot to join, or try rejoining with Instant Join.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Transcription Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No transcription appearing">
    **Try these steps:**
    - Make sure people are speaking in the meeting
    - Check that the bot has been admitted to the meeting
    - Verify the correct language is selected in **Options**
    - Wait a few seconds - there may be a short delay
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transcription quality is poor">
    **Tips to improve accuracy:**
    - Use better microphones (headsets work well)
    - Reduce background noise
    - Speak clearly at a normal pace
    - Avoid multiple people talking at once
    - Make sure the correct language is set in **Options**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong language detected">
    **Solution:** Go to **Options** and set your transcription language manually instead of using "Auto".
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Calendar Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Meetings not showing up">
    **Try these steps:**
    - Make sure your calendar is connected in the **Calendars** tab
    - Check that your meetings have a Google Meet or Teams link
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your calendar
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't connect my calendar">
    **Try these steps:**
    - Make sure you're signing in with the correct account
    - Allow all requested permissions when connecting
    - Try using a different browser
    - Clear your browser cache and try again
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't sign in">
    **Try these steps:**
    - Make sure you're using the same account you signed up with (Google or Microsoft)
    - Clear your browser cache and cookies
    - Try using a different browser
    - Check that you have a stable internet connection
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Access denied error">
    **Solution:** When signing in, make sure you approve all the permission requests. Eagle Noter needs access to your calendar to show your meetings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Upload Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="File upload failing">
    **Check these things:**
    - File must be under 500 MB
    - Use supported formats: MP3, M4A, WAV, MP4, MOV, WEBM, etc.
    - Make sure you have a stable internet connection
    - Try a smaller file to test
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transcription taking too long">
    **Note:** Large files (over 1 hour) may take several minutes to process. You'll see a progress indicator during upload and processing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
