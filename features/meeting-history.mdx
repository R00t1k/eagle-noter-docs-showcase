## Overview

The **Meetings** tab is your central library for all past conversations. Every meeting the bot has joined is saved here, along with the full transcript, AI summary, and recording.

## Viewing Your Meetings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Meetings">
    Click **Meetings** in the sidebar of your workspace dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Your History">
    Meetings are listed chronologically with the most recent first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to View Details">
    Click any meeting to see the full transcript, AI summary, and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Shown for Each Meeting

Each meeting entry displays:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Title** | The meeting name from your calendar or upload |
| **Date & Time** | When the meeting took place |
| **Duration** | How long the meeting lasted |
| **Platform** | Google Meet or Microsoft Teams |
| **Status** | Scheduled, In Progress, or Completed |

## Meeting Details Page

Click on any meeting to view:

### Transcript
The complete text of everything said during the meeting, with:
- Speaker labels (when identified)
- Timestamps
- Full searchable text

### AI Summary
Automatically generated summary including:
- Meeting overview
- Key points discussed
- Decisions made
- Action items
- Next steps

### Audio Playback
Listen to the original meeting recording directly in your browser.

### Export Options
- **SRT** - Download subtitles in SRT format
- **VTT** - Download subtitles in WebVTT format

## Filtering Your History

Find specific meetings by:

- **Date** - Filter by when meetings occurred
- **Platform** - Show only Google Meet or Teams meetings
- **Search** - Use the search feature to find specific content

<Tip>
Use the Search feature to find meetings by what was discussed, not just by title or date.
</Tip>

## Deleting Meetings

To delete a meeting:

1. Go to the meeting you want to delete
2. Click the delete option
3. Confirm the deletion

<Warning>
Deleting a meeting permanently removes the recording, transcript, and AI summary. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
