## Overview

The **Instant Join** feature lets you send the Eagle Noter bot to any active meeting instantly. No calendar integration required - just paste a meeting link and the bot joins immediately.

This is perfect for:
- Ad-hoc meetings that aren't on your calendar
- Quick calls you want to record
- Testing the bot before connecting your calendar
- Joining external meetings you've been invited to

## How to Use Instant Join

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Instant Join">
    Click **Instant Join** in the sidebar of your workspace dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the Meeting Link">
    Copy the full meeting URL and paste it into the input field.
    
    Supported formats:
    - Google Meet: `https://meet.google.com/xxx-xxxx-xxx`
    - Microsoft Teams: `https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/...`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Join">
    Click the **Join Meeting** button. The bot will start connecting immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admit the Bot">
    The meeting host must **admit the bot** from the waiting room or lobby.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Support | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Google Meet | ✅ Full support | Host may need to admit from waiting room |
| Microsoft Teams | ✅ Full support | Host must enable guest access and admit from lobby |

## Bot Status

Once you click Join, you'll see the bot's status update in real-time:

- **Connecting** - Bot is joining the meeting
- **Waiting** - Bot is in the waiting room/lobby
- **Joined** - Bot is in the meeting and recording
- **Transcribing** - Live transcription is active

## What Happens After Joining

While in the meeting:
- The bot records all audio
- Real-time transcription appears in your dashboard
- Speaker identification detects who is talking

After the meeting ends:
- The bot automatically leaves
- AI generates a summary with key points and action items
- Everything is saved to your meeting history
- You receive an email notification (if enabled)

## Tips for Best Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Admit the bot quickly">
    The bot will wait in the lobby/waiting room. Make sure the host admits it promptly so it doesn't miss any content.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verify the meeting has started">
    The bot can only join meetings that are active. Make sure at least one person has already joined the meeting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check your language settings">
    Before joining, make sure your transcription language is set correctly in **Options** for the best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Viewing Instant Join History

All meetings you've joined via Instant Join are saved to your **Meetings** tab. You can:
- View the full transcript
- Read the AI-generated summary
- Export subtitles (SRT/VTT)
- Search across all your instant join sessions

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bot won't join">
    - Make sure the meeting link is correct and complete
    - Verify the meeting is active (someone must be in it)
    - Check that the platform is supported (Google Meet or Teams)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bot stuck in waiting room">
    - The host needs to admit the bot
    - For Teams, guest access must be enabled by the organizer
    - Ask the host to look for the bot in the lobby
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No transcription appearing">
    - Ensure people are speaking in the meeting
    - Check your language settings match the spoken language
    - Give it a few seconds - transcription may have a short delay
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
