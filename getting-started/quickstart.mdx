## Welcome to Eagle Noter!

This quickstart guide will help you set up Eagle Noter and record your first meeting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Go to [eaglenoter.ai](https://eaglenoter.ai) and sign up using your Google or Microsoft account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Workspace">
    After signing in, create your first workspace. Workspaces help you organize meetings by team, project, or purpose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Calendar (Optional)">
    Link your Google Calendar or Microsoft Calendar to see upcoming meetings:
    1. Go to the **Calendars** tab
    2. Click to connect **Google Calendar** or **Microsoft Calendar**
    3. Authorize access to your calendar
    4. You can connect multiple accounts from each provider
    
    <Tip>
      Once connected, your upcoming meetings will appear automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Language Preferences">
    Go to **Options** to configure:
    - **Transcriber Language** - Choose Auto, English, German/Swiss German, or French
    - **AI Summary Language** - Choose the language for your meeting summaries
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join Your First Meeting">
    You have two options:
    
    **From Calendar:**
    - Go to **Calendars** tab
    - Find your meeting
    - Click **Invite Bot**
    
    **Instant Join:**
    - Go to **Instant Join** tab
    - Paste any Google Meet or Teams link
    - Click **Join Meeting**
    
    <Warning>
      Remember: The meeting host must admit the bot from the waiting room/lobby!
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Your Results">
    After the meeting ends:
    - Go to **Meetings** tab
    - Click on your meeting
    - View the full transcript and AI summary
    - Export subtitles (SRT/VTT) if needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Can Do

Once you're set up, here's what Eagle Noter offers:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Real-time Transcription** | See what's being said as the meeting happens |
| **AI Summaries** | Get key points, decisions, and action items automatically |
| **Search** | Find anything said across all your meetings |
| **Upload Recordings** | Transcribe existing audio/video files |
| **Export Subtitles** | Download transcripts as SRT or VTT files |
| **Email Notifications** | Get summaries delivered to your inbox |

## Quick Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I test the bot?">
    Use **Instant Join** to test with any meeting. Start a Google Meet or Teams call yourself, then paste the link into Instant Join to see how it works.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages are supported?">
    Transcription supports:
    - **Auto (Multilingual)** - Automatically detects the language
    - **English**
    - **German/Swiss German**
    - **French**
    
    AI summaries can be generated in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, or Portuguese.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot isn't joining my meeting">
    Make sure:
    - The meeting has started (someone must be in it)
    - The meeting link is correct
    - You admit the bot from the waiting room/lobby
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload existing recordings?">
    Yes! Go to the **Recordings** tab and click **Upload Recording**. You can upload audio or video files up to 500 MB.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
