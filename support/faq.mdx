## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Eagle Noter?">
    Eagle Noter is an AI meeting assistant that joins your Google Meet and Microsoft Teams calls, records the audio, transcribes everything said, and generates intelligent summaries with key points and action items.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which meeting platforms are supported?">
    Eagle Noter currently supports:
    - **Google Meet** - Fully supported
    - **Microsoft Teams** - Fully supported
    
    More platforms coming soon!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages are supported for transcription?">
    Eagle Noter supports the following transcription languages:
    - **Auto (Multilingual)** - Automatically detects the spoken language
    - **English** - Optimized for English
    - **German/Swiss German** - Optimized for German and Swiss German dialects
    - **French** - Optimized for French
    
    You can set your default language in the Options tab of your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages are supported for AI summaries?">
    AI summaries can be generated in:
    - Auto (detects from transcript)
    - English
    - German
    - French
    - Spanish
    - Italian
    - Portuguese
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the bot join my meeting?">
    The Eagle Noter bot joins your meeting as a participant. You can either:
    1. Use **Instant Join** to paste any meeting link
    2. Connect your **Google Calendar** or **Microsoft Calendar** and invite the bot to calendar events
    
    The meeting host needs to admit the bot from the waiting room/lobby.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upload my own recordings?">
    Yes! Go to the **Recordings** tab to upload audio or video files. Supported formats include MP3, M4A, WAV, MP4, MOV, and more (up to 500 MB).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export transcriptions?">
    Yes! Transcriptions can be exported as:
    - **SRT** - Subtitle file format
    - **VTT** - Web Video Text Tracks format
    
    Click the Export button on any meeting to download subtitles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I search across all my meetings?">
    Yes! Use the Search feature to find specific words, phrases, or topics across all your meeting transcripts and summaries.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does the AI summary include?">
    AI summaries include:
    - Overall meeting summary
    - Key points discussed
    - Decisions made
    - Action items and next steps
    - Sentiment analysis
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We adhere to strict security standards to protect your meeting data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can see my meetings?">
    Only you and members of your workspace can see your meetings. Meetings are private to your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete my data?">
    Yes. You can delete individual recordings, transcripts, and summaries at any time. Deleting a meeting removes all associated data permanently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the bot read meeting chat?">
    No. The bot only captures and processes audio. Meeting chat messages are not recorded or stored.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The bot won't join my meeting">
    - Make sure the meeting has started (at least one person must be in it)
    - Verify the meeting link is correct and complete
    - Check that the platform is supported (Google Meet or Teams)
    - For Teams, ensure guest access is enabled
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot is stuck in the waiting room">
    The meeting host needs to admit the bot. Look for a notification in the meeting interface showing someone is waiting to join.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transcription quality is poor">
    - Check that the correct language is selected in Options
    - Use better microphones
    - Reduce background noise
    - Ensure speakers don't talk over each other
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Workspace

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I invite team members?">
    Go to your workspace settings and use the invite feature to add team members by email. They'll receive an invitation to join your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple workspaces?">
    Yes! You can create multiple workspaces to organize meetings by project, team, or client.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
