## Overview

After every meeting, Eagle Noter automatically generates an AI-powered summary that captures the key points, decisions made, and action items. No more manual note-taking or missing important details.

## What's Included in a Summary

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Meeting Summary" icon="file-lines">
    A concise overview of what was discussed
  </Card>
  <Card title="Key Points" icon="list">
    The main topics and important discussion points
  </Card>
  <Card title="Decisions Made" icon="gavel">
    Key decisions reached during the meeting
  </Card>
  <Card title="Action Items" icon="check">
    Tasks and follow-ups that were assigned
  </Card>

## How It Works

1. **Meeting ends** - The bot leaves and processing begins
2. **Transcript analyzed** - AI reads through the full transcript
3. **Summary generated** - Key information is extracted and organized
4. **Email sent** - You receive the summary in your inbox (if enabled)
5. **Available in dashboard** - View the full summary anytime

<Note>
Summary generation typically takes 1-2 minutes after the meeting ends.
</Note>

## Summary Languages

You can configure which language your AI summaries are generated in. Go to **Options** in your workspace:

| Language | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Auto | Automatically matches the transcript language |
| English | Summaries always in English |
| German | Summaries always in German |
| French | Summaries always in French |
| Spanish | Summaries always in Spanish |
| Italian | Summaries always in Italian |
| Portuguese | Summaries always in Portuguese |

<Tip>
Using "Auto" will detect the language from your transcript and generate the summary in that language.
</Tip>

## Viewing Summaries

Access your meeting summaries:

1. Go to **Meetings** in your dashboard
2. Click on any completed meeting
3. The AI summary appears alongside the full transcript

You can also:
- **Search** summaries across all your meetings
- **Share** summaries with workspace members
- **Export** summaries with your transcript

## Email Notifications

Get summaries delivered to your inbox automatically:

1. Go to **Options** in your workspace
2. Find **Email Notifications**
3. Enable summary emails
4. Optionally add CC recipients

<Tip>
Add your team's email addresses as CC recipients so everyone gets the meeting summary.
</Tip>

## Tips for Better Summaries

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Speak clearly">
    Better transcription leads to better summaries. Clear audio and distinct speakers help the AI understand what was discussed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Be explicit about action items">
    When assigning tasks, say things like "John will handle the report by Friday" - this helps the AI identify action items.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Summarize decisions verbally">
    When a decision is made, stating "So we've decided to..." helps the AI capture it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
