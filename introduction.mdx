## Your AI Meeting Assistant

Eagle Noter is your intelligent companion for online meetings. Our bot joins your Google Meet and Microsoft Teams calls, records the audio, and uses advanced AI to transcribe and summarize everything that happens - so you can focus on the conversation.

### What Eagle Noter Does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Joins Your Meetings" icon="robot">
    Our bot joins Google Meet and Teams calls as a participant
  </Card>
  <Card title="Records & Transcribes" icon="microphone">
    High-quality audio recording with real-time transcription
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Summaries" icon="sparkles">
    Automatic summaries with key points, decisions, and action items
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search Everything" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Find anything said across all your meetings
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Why Eagle Noter?

- **Never Take Notes Again** - Focus on the conversation while we capture every detail
- **Multi-Language Support** - Transcription in English, German/Swiss German, French, or auto-detect
- **Find Anything** - Search across all your past meetings to find exactly what was said
- **Actionable Insights** - Get summaries, action items, and decisions delivered to your inbox

## Getting Started

New to Eagle Noter? Start here:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with Google or Microsoft at [eaglenoter.ai](https://eaglenoter.ai)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Calendar">
    Link your Google Calendar to see upcoming meetings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite the Bot">
    Click "Invite Bot" on any meeting - or use Instant Join for any meeting link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Summary">
    After the meeting, view your transcript and AI-generated summary
  </Step>
</Steps>
