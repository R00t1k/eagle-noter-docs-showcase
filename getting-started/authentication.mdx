## Overview

Eagle Noter supports secure sign-in using your existing Google or Microsoft accounts. You do not need to create a separate password.

## How to Sign In

1.  Go to the [Eagle Noter Login Page](https://eaglenoter.ai/auth).
2.  Choose your preferred provider:
    - **Continue with Google**: Best for Gmail or Google Workspace users.
    - **Continue with Microsoft**: Best for Outlook or Office 365 users.
3.  Follow the prompts to authorize your account.

## Why Connect Your Account?

Connecting your Google or Microsoft account allows Eagle Noter to:

- **Sync your Calendar**: Automatically find and join your scheduled meetings.
- **Secure Access**: Use enterprise-grade security from Google/Microsoft.

## Troubleshooting

### "Access Denied"

If you see an access denied error, please ensure you have approved the necessary permissions for Eagle Noter to access your calendar.

### "Account Not Found"

If you are a new user, signing in will automatically create an account for you.
